Figures: Coverage on TSS
========================

We computed coverage per nucleosome. For each nucleosomes we computed the normalized enrichment among high, medium, low, and silent genes. We benchmark our MNChIP-seq libraries against the known function of H3K4me3, H3K27Ac, H3K9me3, and H3K27me3. We looked at the enrichment of these histone marks around transcription start sites (TSS).
